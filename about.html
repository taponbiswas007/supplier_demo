Our Journey Since 2008

PrimeSource Supply was founded with a simple mission: to provide retailers with access to high-quality wholesale products at competitive prices. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted supplier for businesses across North America.

Our founder, Michael Rodriguez, recognized the challenges that small and medium-sized retailers faced in sourcing reliable products. With his background in retail management, he established PrimeSource to bridge the gap between manufacturers and retailers.

Today, we pride ourselves on maintaining the personal touch of a family business while leveraging industry expertise to provide exceptional value to our partners.

Michael Rodriguez

Founder & CEO
